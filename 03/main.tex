\title{Quiz 3}

\maketitle

Decide whether the following formula are provable or not provable. You don't 
have to provide a proof. 

\begin{enumerate}

\item $\neg (A \land \neg A)$

\item $(A \to B) \to B$. 

\item $\neg A \land A \to B$ 

\end{enumerate}
